## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Keep it simple**: Investors skim decks in 3-4 minutes
2. **One idea per slide**: Don't overcrowd
3. **Visual >> Text**: Use images, charts, icons over paragraphs
4. **Tell a story**: Problem → Solution → Traction → Ask
5. **Numbers matter**: Be specific with metrics
6. **Practice your pitch**: Deck is support material, not the presentation
7. **Have a demo ready**: Link or video embedded
8. **Prepare appendix**: Detailed financials, tech specs, case studies
